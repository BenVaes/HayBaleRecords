ABOUT US



Hi!


We are Hay Bale Records, a Bluegrass Record Label.


This label got established by 4 young people: Ben Vaes, Yannis Van der Meulen, Ben Raedts and Jaimy Boermans. Our goal is to make Bluegrass music more popular for the new generations and bring people together.

We also like to put up and coming bands in the spotlight. They can vary from young to old, because we believe Bluegrass is a music genre that can be enjoyed by everyone.
